# Gut Diary — Digestive Health Tracker

A calm, honest journal for tracking gut health, digestive symptoms, and food
reactions. Log meals with photos, track bowel movements on the Bristol Stool
Scale, identify food-symptom patterns, and share a clinician report at your
next appointment — all without your data ever leaving your device.

## What it does

- **Gut health profile.** A short onboarding quiz sets up your profile:
  conditions (IBS, IBD, Crohn's, UC, GERD, Coeliac, SIBO, and more), diet
  type, primary goal, and whether you've had a formal diagnosis. No account
  needed.
- **10 log types, one tap each.** Food & meals, symptoms, bowel movements,
  water intake, medications & supplements, mood & stress, exercise, sleep,
  bloating, and gas — all accessible from the Log tab.
- **Food photo journal.** Capture a photo of every meal, scan a barcode for
  instant product lookup, note portion size and meal type, and add free-text
  notes. Your food history builds into a searchable timeline.
- **Bowel movement log.** Bristol Stool Scale types 1–7, colour classification,
  urgency rating, and flags for blood or mucus present. Clinical-grade
  detail for derm or gastroenterologist visits.
- **Symptom log.** A 1–10 severity slider plus symptom tags (bloating, cramps,
  nausea, reflux, fatigue, gas, constipation, diarrhoea, pain, and more).
- **Trigger correlation engine.** Looks across your food and symptom logs to
  surface the food-symptom pairs that appear most often, with a confidence
  score and typical delay. No AI inference — only pattern matching on your
  own data. (Pro)
- **FODMAP database lookup.** Traffic-light FODMAP ratings by portion size,
  directly inside the food log. (Pro)
- **Apple Health integration.** Read sleep duration and steps automatically;
  write water intake back to Health. (Pro)
- **Apple Watch quick-logging.** Log symptoms, water, and mood from your wrist.
  (Pro)
- **Clinician export.** Generate a 30-day or 90-day PDF report covering
  symptoms, bowel trends, food logs, and adherence — ready to hand to your
  gastroenterologist or dietitian. (Pro)
- **Adherence & streak tracking.** Daily streaks with a forgiving cadence so
  one missed day doesn't reset your progress.

### Free includes

- All 10 log types — unlimited entries
- Food photo capture and barcode scanning
- Bowel movement log with Bristol Stool Scale
- Symptom log with severity and tags
- Medication and supplement tracking
- Mood, stress, exercise, water, sleep, bloating, and gas logging
- Journal calendar view — last 30 days of history
- Basic charts: bowel summary, mood impact, monthly trend, day-of-week patterns

- Clinician export — 30-day and 90-day PDF reports + CSV

## Photo analysis

The App offers an optional **Analyze photo** action that submits a single
photo to our Cloudflare Worker for severity scoring. By tapping that
action you instruct us to process that photo. The Worker returns a
structured result and discards the photo; it is not stored, logged, or
used to train any model. Photos are never uploaded automatically.

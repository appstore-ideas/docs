# Privacy Policy

*Effective date: 2026-05-13*

## Summary

Skincare Routine is built to keep your data on your device. Your products,
routines, check-ins, and progress photos never leave your phone. The only third
parties involved are Apple (for the App Store and StoreKit) and RevenueCat
(which verifies your purchase status).

## Data we store on your device

The following information is created and stored only on your iPhone, in the
app's private storage:

- Skin type and goals you selected during onboarding
- Display name (if you choose to enter one)
- Products you add to your routine, including ingredients you provide
- Daily check-ins (which AM/PM routines you marked as done)
- Progress photos you capture for the journal
- App preferences (language, notification toggles, theme)

We do not transmit any of this information to our servers, because we don't run
any servers for the app. If you delete the app, this data is removed with it.

## Purchases

When you make a purchase inside Skincare Routine, the transaction is processed
by Apple's StoreKit using your Apple ID. We never see your payment details.

To verify your purchase status across reinstalls and devices, we use
[RevenueCat](https://www.revenuecat.com/) as a sub-processor. RevenueCat
receives an anonymous, randomly generated identifier (an "app user ID") and the
receipt data Apple provides. It does not receive your name, email, Apple ID, or
any of the on-device data described above. RevenueCat's privacy practices are
documented at [revenuecat.com/privacy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy).

## What we do not collect

- We do not collect analytics, usage telemetry, or crash reports.
- We do not track you across other apps or websites.
- We do not use advertising identifiers.
- We do not sell or share data with third parties for marketing.
- We do not require sign-in to use the app.

## Permissions the app may request

- **Camera** — to scan product barcodes and capture progress photos. The
  camera feed is processed locally and never streamed off-device.
- **Photo library** — only when you pick a photo for the journal.
- **Notifications** — for AM/PM reminders, the weekly photo prompt, and
  streak notifications. All schedules are local. You can disable any of these
  in the app's Reminders screen.

## Children

Skincare Routine is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly
collect information from children.

## Your rights

Because the app does not collect personal data on a server, there is no
server-side data to access, export, or delete. You can remove all of your
on-device data by deleting the app. For purchase records held by RevenueCat or
Apple, contact us using the email below and we will assist with deletion
requests subject to RevenueCat's and Apple's policies.

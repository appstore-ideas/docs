# Acne Tracker — Skin Journal

A gentle, honest acne journal. Take a 90-second skin quiz, get a personalized
AM/PM routine, mark each day's check-in done, and watch a same-position photo
timeline tell the story. No AI face scan. No fake severity score. No
"pay per scan."

## What it does

- **Skin quiz.** Seven questions (skin type, breakout zones, treatments,
  sensitivities, goals) build your personalized AM/PM routine.
- **AM and PM check-ins.** One-tap log with a forgiving "did you do PM last
  night?" backfill so you don't lose your streak from a single missed log.
- **Same-position photo timeline.** Front-facing camera with a face-positioning
  oval and a ghost overlay of your last shot. Deterministic alignment — no
  face scan, no AI hallucinating spots.
- **Streak with grace.** Late-night grace until 3 AM, calendar with the
  current and previous month, gentle copy when life gets in the way.
- **Trigger journal.** Stress, sleep, dairy, sugar, cycle — only if you opt
  in during onboarding.
- **Ingredient + barcode scanner.** Scans products against your routine and
  flags conflicts ("may not be ideal" — never "BAD FOR YOUR SKIN").

# Hair Routine

A calm, on-device hair-care scheduler. Build your weekly wash days, treatments,
and refresh days, mark them done, and watch the streak grow. Capture weekly
progress photos with a simple positioning guide. No sign-in, no servers, no
analysis of your hair.

## What it does

- **Weekly routine builder.** Set up wash days, treatment days, oil days,
  refresh days, and rest days — your schedule, your rules.
- **One-tap check-in.** Mark each day's task done. Forgot yesterday? Backfill
  it without losing your streak.
- **Product tracking.** Add the products you use, see which ones get used and
  which collect dust.
- **Photo journal.** Front-camera capture with an oval positioning guide so
  your photos stay consistent week to week. All photos stay on your device.
- **Streak calendar.** A month-grid view of every routine day you've completed,
  with monthly streak freezes that auto-protect a missed day.

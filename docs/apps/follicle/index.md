# Follicle — Hair Transplant & Loss Tracker

A calm, honest journal for men tracking hair transplant recovery or hair loss
treatment. Take a quick hair profile quiz, log your daily treatments, capture
3-angle progress photos, and watch a same-position timeline tell the story of
your recovery. No AI face scan. No fabricated density scores. Your photos are
always free to view — no paywall, ever.

## What it does

- **Hair profile quiz.** A few questions (Norwood stage, treatment history,
  primary goal, transplant details if applicable) set up your personal profile
  and treatment plan.
- **Daily treatment log.** One-tap check-in for each treatment — Minoxidil,
  Finasteride, supplements, PRP, and more. Streak tracking with a forgiving
  cadence so a single missed day doesn't wipe your progress.
- **Daily check-in.** 0–10 sliders for shedding intensity and scalp itch, plus
  a free-text notes field. All data stays on your device.
- **3-angle photo timeline.** Front, left, and right captures with a ghost
  overlay of your previous session for consistent framing. No face scan, no
  biometric matching — just deterministic alignment.
- **Transplant milestone engine.** 9 rule-based milestone cards keyed off your
  surgery date: scab clearing (day 10), shock loss warning (month 1), the shed
  (month 3 — the most important card), first growth (month 4), and more. Calm,
  factual copy written with transplant patients in mind.
- **Side-by-side compare.** Place any two sessions next to each other at the
  same angle to see real change over time.
- **Adherence & shedding insights.** 30-day trend charts for treatment
  adherence, shedding score, and scalp itch. No AI inference — only your
  own logged data.
